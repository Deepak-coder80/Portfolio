About Me

I am a constantly growing engineer passionate about building clean, efficient, and scalable software. I actively contribute to the Python ecosystem and have built two open-source Python packages: env_factory and sm_env_read.

These tools help manage environment configurations and secrets in a structured and secure manner across multiple environments, utilizing Python, setuptools, pip packaging, environment variable handling, secrets management, and comprehensive testing using Pytest.

I bring a business-first mindset to engineering, with strength in building reliable systems while aligning technical solutions with business goals. I enjoy architecting systems, mentoring teams, and ensuring delivery excellence.
